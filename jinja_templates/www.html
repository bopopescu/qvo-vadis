Sharing and embedding
 Use the URL to share your information in three simple steps:
  1. Go to hash.maptiming.com and navigate the map, filter the events or select a location
  2. Copy the URL from your browser and share it with your friends
  3. Embed the map on your website as HTML iframe, using the same URL. Check this jsfiddle for an example
To share a map that shows the user's current location, remove the coordinates from the URL:
http://hash.maptiming.com/#51.176542,4.458353,13z,671px/now/label/hash/tag
http://hash.maptiming.com/#now/label/hash/tag
